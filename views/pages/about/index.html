About Hannah D. Foster


Originally from North Carolina, Hannah Foster is a family and lifestyle photographer based in the Northern Virginia area. Hannah has had the passion for creativity and telling stories through art since before she can remember. She founded HDF Photography after working many years as an actress and performer. As a storyteller herself, she loves the opportunity to meet her clients and capture some of life's most beautiful moments. Whether you are engaged, a mother to-be, getting married, or gathering the family for portraits, she puts her heart and soul into crafting images that will be treasured for years to come. When she's not behind the camera, Hannah fills her time with her love for music, traveling abroad, and running.
